— About Us —

Blarnify is a small new media & communications studio founded by designer, writer and political communications specialist Ian Patrick “Pat” Hines.

Pat lives & breathes the Internet. In 2003–04, he managed the GenDean Blog for Howard Dean — whose trailblazing online campaign set the stage for Barack Obama's 2008 effort.

Following college, he spent four years in Baltimore, first as a 5th-grade teacher in one of the city's most challenged schools and later in City Hall, where he worked with community leaders to make government more responsive and accountable.

In 2010 he was recruited by the Governor's Office to become a spokesman for the State of Maryland, first on energy issues and most recently served as director of communications for the Maryland Department of Human Resources.

An experienced manager with real on-the-record experience and a passion for digital media, Pat founded Blarnify to help non-profit and advocacy organizations, political campaigns and small businesses leverage the power of the internet to achieve their goals.

You can find him online at ianpatrickhines.com.
